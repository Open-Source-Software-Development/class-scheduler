Detailed Description

A pair of two elements.

Like java.util.Collection the Collections framework},

Pair

redefines equals(Object) to care only about the elements of the pair, not the specific implementation. Consult the documentation for equals(Object) hashCode() for further information.

Parameters
<L>the type of the left-hand element
